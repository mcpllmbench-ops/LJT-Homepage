**Authors:** Yuzhen Huang, Yuzhuo Bai, Zhihao Zhu, Junlei Zhang, Jinghan Zhang, Tangjun Su, Junteng Liu, Chuancheng Lv, Yikai Zhang, Jiayi Lei, Yao Fu, Maosong Sun, Junxian He

**Abstract:** This paper introduces C-Eval, a comprehensive evaluation suite designed to assess foundation models' capabilities across multiple levels and disciplines in Chinese. The evaluation covers various cognitive abilities and knowledge domains, providing a standardized benchmark for Chinese language understanding.

**Keywords:** Chinese Evaluation, Foundation Models, Benchmark, Multi-Level Assessment, Language Understanding
