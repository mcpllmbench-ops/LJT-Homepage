**Authors:** Shiqi Chen, Miao Xiong, Junteng Liu, Zhengxuan Wu, Teng Xiao, Siyang Gao, Junxian He

**Abstract:** This paper introduces a novel approach to hallucination mitigation in large language models by analyzing in-context sharpness from an inner representation perspective. We propose using sharpness as alerts to identify and mitigate hallucinations during model inference.

**Keywords:** Hallucination Mitigation, Large Language Models, In-Context Learning, Model Interpretability, Representation Learning
