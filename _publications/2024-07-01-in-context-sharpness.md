Co-authored research on using in-context sharpness as alerts for hallucination mitigation in LLMs. This work provides a novel perspective on detecting and mitigating hallucinations through analysis of internal representations.

**Authors:** Shiqi Chen, Miao Xiong, Junteng Liu, Zhengxuan Wu, Teng Xiao, Siyang Gao, Junxian He
