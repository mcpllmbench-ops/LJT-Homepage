**Authors:** Junteng Liu, Weihao Zeng, Xiwen Zhang, Yijun Wang, Zifei Shan, Junxian He

**Abstract:** This paper analyzes the perception bottlenecks in Vision-Language Models (VLMs) when applied to chart understanding tasks. We identify key limitations in current VLM architectures and propose methods to improve their performance on visual data interpretation tasks.

**Keywords:** Vision-Language Models, Chart Understanding, Perception Bottleneck, Visual Reasoning
