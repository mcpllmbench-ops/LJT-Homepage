First author publication on synthesizing verifiable reasoning data for logical reasoning tasks. This work presents a comprehensive approach to generating large-scale, verifiable reasoning datasets for training and evaluating logical reasoning capabilities in language models.

**Authors:** Junteng Liu, Yuanxiang Fan, Zhuo Jiang, Han Ding, Yongyi Hu, Chi Zhang, Yiqi Shi, Shitong Weng, Aili Chen, Shiqi Chen, Yunan Huang, Mozhi Zhang, Pengyu Zhao, Junjie Yan, Junxian He
