Co-authored comprehensive evaluation suite for Chinese foundation models. This work presents a systematic approach to evaluating Chinese language understanding and generation capabilities across multiple domains and difficulty levels.

**Authors:** Yuzhen Huang, Yuzhuo Bai, Zhihao Zhu, Junlei Zhang, Jinghan Zhang, Tangjun Su, Junteng Liu, Chuancheng Lv, Yikai Zhang, Jiayi Lei, Yao Fu, Maosong Sun, Junxian He
