**Authors:** Junteng Liu, Shiqi Chen, Yu Cheng, Junxian He

**Abstract:** This paper investigates the existence and properties of a universal truthfulness hyperplane inside large language models. We provide theoretical analysis and empirical evidence for this concept, demonstrating its implications for understanding and improving model truthfulness.

**Keywords:** Large Language Models, Truthfulness, Interpretability, Model Understanding, Neural Representations
