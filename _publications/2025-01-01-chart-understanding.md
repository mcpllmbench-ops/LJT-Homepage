First author investigation into perception bottlenecks in vision-language models for chart understanding tasks. This research identifies key limitations in current VLM architectures when processing and understanding chart visualizations.

**Authors:** Junteng Liu, Weihao Zeng, Xiwen Zhang, Yijun Wang, Zifei Shan, Junxian He
