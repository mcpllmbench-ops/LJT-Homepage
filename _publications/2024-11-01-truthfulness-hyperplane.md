First author research on universal truthfulness hyperplane inside large language models. This work explores the geometric properties of truthfulness in LLM representations and proposes methods for truthfulness detection and improvement.

**Authors:** Junteng Liu, Shiqi Chen, Yu Cheng, Junxian He
